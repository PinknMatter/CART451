Week 1

Self - Bio

It's always been a struggle for me, to write a biography about myself, you see. How can a few sentences capture the complexities of my nature? I'm not sure what to say, mostly because I don't like writing about me. Sometimes it feels like boasting or I can't do myself justice, you'll agree. I can describe who I am right now, but the future is a mystery. I'm still discovering who I want to be, and what my life's true purpose will be.
